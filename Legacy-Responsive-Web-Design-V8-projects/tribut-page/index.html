Dr. Cerep Cerepovic

The woman who saved many lives

Here's a time line of Dr. Cerep's life:

  • 1983: Born in Umpa near Lumpa
  • 1990: Cared for wild dogs in her magical village.
  • 2000: Built a rescue network in the forest of Glimmerhain.
  • 2020: Founded "Paws of Hope" and saved thousands of dogs worldwide.
  • 2021: Disappeared mysteriously; believed to still rescue animals in secret.
"Where the cries of the voiceless echo, I will be there—unseen but never absent."
